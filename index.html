Our Story

Twenty Years
in the Making,
One Dough at a Time

Before there was a restaurant, there were two decades of kitchens, dining rooms, and late-night conversations about what pizza could be.

Chef André Barone and Daphne Glidden met in 2001 and spent the next twenty years moving between New York, Miami, and São Paulo — him sharpening his craft across restaurants and product development, her directing luxury events for VIP clients and earning a Master’s in Italian Food & Oenology from the ICIF school in Piedmont, Italy. Eight months living in a 14th-century castle, training under Michelin-starred chefs, studying breadmaking, pizza, focaccia, and wine pairing. She came home fluent in four languages and certain about one thing: great food is about patience, not shortcuts.

This isn't pizza trying to be fancy. It's Neapolitan tradition filtered through São Paulo soul — calabresa smoked in-house, catupiry shipped from Brazil, and a dough that's been rising longer than most people's commute. Every pie tells the story of where we've been and who we cook for.

André & Daphne — est. 2022

In 2020, the world stopped — and their dream finally started.

Working from home, they launched The Doughfellas as a line of frozen pizzas made entirely from scratch: 72-hour slow-fermented dough, hand-picked ingredients, no compromises. Word spread fast. The frozen pies were so good that people started asking where the restaurant was.

So in 2022, they opened one — a small family-run pizzeria on Federal Highway in Pompano Beach, where André works the oven himself and Daphne makes sure every guest is treated like they're sitting at their own dinner table.

What Goes Into Every Pie

The Good Stuff

We don't use the word "premium" lightly. Every ingredient earns its place on the pie — hand-picked, sourced with intention, and treated with respect. No fillers. No substitutes. Just the real thing.

🫓
The 72-Hour Dough
Our slow-fermented signature
Three full days of slow fermentation. No rush, no shortcuts. The long rise develops a complex flavor and that signature leopard-spotted char on the crust. Light inside, crisp outside.
made in-house daily
🧀
Grana Padano DOP
Aged 12+ months — Po Valley, Italy
DOP-certified Italian hard cheese aged over a year in the Po River Valley. Nuttier and more delicate than Parmigiano, it finishes every pie with a savory depth that ties the toppings together.
DOP certified
🫕
Catupiry
The cream cheese of São Paulo
Brazil's beloved creamy cheese, tangy and rich — a staple on pizzas across São Paulo. You won't find it at most American pizzerias. We use it because Chef André grew up on it, and once you try it, you'll understand why.
Brazilian soul
🌿
Fresh Mozzarella
Pulled fresh, never frozen
Soft, milky, and made to melt. Our fresh mozzarella arrives whole and gets pulled by hand for each service. It's the difference between pizza that tastes like cheese and pizza that tastes like milk, cream, and salt in perfect balance.
hand-pulled
🔥
Calabresa Sausage
Smoked in-house — Brazilian-style
We smoke our own calabresa right here. It's a coarser, more aromatic sausage than Italian varieties — paprika-forward, lightly smoky, and sliced thick. The kind of thing that makes our Calabresa pizza impossible to replicate elsewhere.
smoked in-house
🫒
Kalamata Olives
Brined whole — Peloponnese, Greece
Dark, meaty, and almond-shaped. Kalamatas bring a rich, wine-like brininess that cuts through the richness of melted cheese. We use them whole and slice in-house — never the pre-pitted, mushy kind.
whole brined
🍅
The Tomato Sauce
Made from scratch, every batch
San Marzano-style tomatoes, crushed and simmered with garlic and fresh basil. No sugar added, no paste, no concentrate. Just ripe tomatoes cooked down until they taste like summer.
made in-house
🐝
Hot Honey Drizzle
Sweet heat, finished tableside
A finishing drizzle of honey infused with chili — the sweet-spicy contrast that turns our Pepperoni & Ricotta pizza into something addictive. Applied after the oven so the honey stays liquid and glossy.
finishing touch
🫧
Truffle Oil
Extra virgin, infused — for the White Pizza
A few drops of truffle-infused extra virgin olive oil transform our White Pizza from great to unforgettable. Earthy, aromatic, and used with restraint — because truffle oil should whisper, not shout.
finishing touch
Zero shortcuts ✦
